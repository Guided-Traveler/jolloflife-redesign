Who We Are

Bringing West Africa to Winnipeg

Jollof Life is your one-stop shop for delicious, authentic African cuisine right here in Winnipeg. We believe food is more than fuel — it is culture, connection, and community on a plate. Every dish we serve carries the rich culinary heritage of Nigeria and West Africa, prepared by experienced hands with time-honored recipes.

Our philosophy is simple: Eat Healthily, Deliver Strength. We source the freshest ingredients, combine them with authentic West African spices, and prepare everything fresh daily. From our signature Jollof Rice to hearty Egusi Soup and smoky Suya, each dish is crafted to nourish the body and warm the soul.

More than a restaurant, Jollof Life is a gathering place — a piece of home for the West African diaspora and an open door for anyone ready to explore the bold, vibrant flavors of Nigerian cooking. We are proud to serve our community from 600 Portage Ave and to share our culture through every meal.

Our Heritage

A Taste of Nigeria in Winnipeg

Nigerian food is one of the most vibrant and diverse cuisines on the planet. From the smoky heat of Suya to the comforting richness of Egusi, every dish tells a story of tradition, family, and celebration. In Nigeria, food is how we welcome strangers, honour elders, and mark life's milestones.

At Jollof Life, we bring that spirit to Manitoba. We believe that sharing a meal is the most powerful way to build bridges between cultures. Whether you grew up with the taste of Jollof on your tongue or you are trying it for the very first time, our table is your table. We celebrate West African heritage in every ingredient, every recipe, and every plate we serve.

Food brings people together. That is the heart of what we do — creating a space where community gathers, culture is celebrated, and every bite connects you to the bold, beautiful flavors of West Africa.
