Our Signature

Exclusive Ofada

Our crown jewel — locally sourced Ofada rice served with our signature ayamase (designer stew). This rich, peppery sauce made with green peppers, locust beans, and palm oil creates an unforgettable flavor combination.

Whether you're a first-timer or a lifelong fan of Nigerian cuisine, our Exclusive Ofada is the dish that keeps guests coming back.
